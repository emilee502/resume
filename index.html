Work Experience

Every Summer I work as a Conversation Pal for Compass USA for a three week homestay.

About 120 Japanese high school students come to Spokane and stay with American host families.

While they are here, they attend English class Monday through Friday.

The 120 students are divided into groups of 20 for these classes.

In each class, there are five American students who help the exchange students with their conversational english.

After three years of working with this program, I applied for a scholarship to go to Chiba, Japan for twelve days. I was awarded the scholarship and stayed with a host family while I attended a Japanese high school.
